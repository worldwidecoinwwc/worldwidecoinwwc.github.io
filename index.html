Structured for Fair Access and Long-Term Stability

WorldWideCoin operates under a fully transparent mining-only distribution framework. There are no founder allocations, no premine reserves, and no public or private token sales. Supply enters circulation exclusively through decentralized Proof-of-Work validation, ensuring every coin is earned rather than pre-assigned.

WWC is designed to be CPU-friendly, allowing participation from standard computers without requiring specialized ASIC hardware. This lowers entry barriers, reduces centralization risk, and enables broader global participation.

The 2.5% continuous annual emission decay model provides a predictable and gradual reduction in block rewards, avoiding disruptive halving shocks while supporting long-term network security and controlled monetary expansion.
