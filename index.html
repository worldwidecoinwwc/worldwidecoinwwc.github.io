About WWC

WWC introduces a 2.5% annual continuous exponential decay model, targeting an asymptotic supply near 21 million coins. The design ensures smooth emission reduction without disruptive halving shocks.

Core Features

Mining Only

No premine. No ICO. Fair distribution through CPU-based Proof-of-Work.

2.5% Decay Model

Continuous emission decay replacing traditional halving systems.

Fee Burn

20% of transaction fees are burned to introduce deflationary pressure.

Decentralized Treasury

5% block reward allocated for sustainable ecosystem development.
